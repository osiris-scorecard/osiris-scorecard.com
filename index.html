What we do

Trust in a noisy and fast paced information environment

During major conflicts, social media accounts become primary news sources for millions of people. Journalists, researchers, and the public cite them regularly. It can be nearly impossible for individuals to keep track of which accounts are reliable. Its also not as simple as x account is good and y account is bad.

The purpose of OSIRIS is to help you know how reliable the accounts you see really are. We rate these accounts independently. Every rating is based on a scored sample of posts, with the methodology published openly.
